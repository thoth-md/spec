# Thoth

Markdown with a few thoughtful additions for scale: composition across files, a bit more syntax instead of HTML, and references to external data. Readable as source. Forgiving by default. Designed for the way we write now, with humans and machines both reading along.

This repository holds the spec, in progress. The shape is taking form in public — drafts, proposals, open questions, and the conversation that surrounds them.

## Join the conversation

Bring ideas, questions, and gripes to [Discussions](../../discussions). We'd rather hear them early than late.

## What we're trying to do

Keep what makes markdown work, and extend it just enough for the cases authors keep reaching past it. Markdown's strength has always been its flexibility — content isn't code, and most authors aren't programmers — and Thoth aims to honor that.

---

Thoth is in active development, built in the open by the team behind Hyroglyph.
